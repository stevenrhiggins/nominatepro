Sample Scoring Rubric Text

List Style

  • 5 Points (Exceptional): The nominee's work has a profound and measurable impact, significantly benefiting a large number of people or the community. Their contributions have long-lasting and widespread effects.
  • 4 Points (Very Good): The nominee's work has a notable impact, with clear benefits that are well-documented. Their contributions have a significant positive effect on the field or community.
  • 3 Points (Good): The nominee's work has a moderate impact, with some benefits but may lack comprehensive evidence. Their contributions are beneficial but not transformative.
  • 2 Points (Fair): The nominee's work has limited impact, with minimal benefits or unclear outcomes. Their contributions have some positive effects but are not substantial.
  • 1 Point (Poor): The nominee's work has little to no impact, with negligible benefits or no evidence of positive outcomes. Their contributions are not impactful.
