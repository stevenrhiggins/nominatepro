Using Numeric Questions to Evaluate Nominations
To ensure a consistent and fair evaluation process, please follow these simple instructions when using the numeric questions to score nominations:

Understand the Scale:

  • Each criterion will be scored on a scale provided.

Read the Nomination Materials:

  • Thoroughly review the nomination form, letters of support, and any additional documents provided for each nominee.

Evaluate Each Criterion:

  • or each criterion, assign a score based on the scale provided. Be objective and consider the evidence provided in the nomination materials.

Assign Scores Carefully:

  • Click on the numeric value or use the slider (if available) to assign your score for each criterion.
  • Make sure to select the score that best represents your evaluation.

Review Your Scores:

  • Before submitting your evaluation, double-check that you have assigned scores for all criteria and that they accurately reflect your assessment of the nomination.

By following these instructions, you will help maintain a consistent and unbiased evaluation process. Thank you for your careful consideration and time in reviewing the nominations.
