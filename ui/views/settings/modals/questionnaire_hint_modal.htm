Sample Questionnaire Instructions Text

Please read and follow these instructions carefully to ensure the accurate and complete submission of your nomination:

  • Purpose:The questionnaire section is designed to help the selection committee understand the nominee's qualifications, achievements, and contributions relevant to the award or recognition.
  • Eligibility:Ensure that the nominee meets the eligibility criteria for the specific award or recognition before filling out the questionnaire.
  • Accuracy:Provide accurate and up-to-date information. Verify all details before submission.
  • Completeness:Answer all questions thoroughly. Incomplete forms may not be considered.
  • Clarity:Use clear and concise language. Avoid jargon and ensure that your responses are easily understandable.
  • Evidence:Where possible, provide specific examples, data, or references to support your responses. This helps to substantiate the nominee's achievements and contributions.
  • Confidentiality:The information provided will be kept confidential and used solely for the purpose of evaluating the nomination.
