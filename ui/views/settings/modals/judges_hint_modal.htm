Judges Evaluation Requirements
We are grateful for your expertise and time in serving as a judge for the [Event/Award Name] nominations. To ensure a fair, transparent, and comprehensive evaluation process, please review the following requirements and guidelines for assessing the nominations assigned to you.

Confidentiality:

  • All nomination materials are confidential. Please do not disclose or discuss the contents of any nomination with others who are not on the judging panel.
  • Maintain the anonymity of the nominees and nominators throughout the evaluation process.

Conflict of Interest:

  • If you have a conflict of interest with a nominee (e.g., family member, close colleague, business partner), please recuse yourself from evaluating that specific nomination and inform the awards coordinator.

Scoring and Comments:

  • Assign an overall score for each nomination as described in the questions/instructions we have provided.
  • Provide clear, concise, and constructive comments explaining your evaluation and offering feedback on the nominee's strengths and areas for improvement where necessary.

Fairness and Impartiality:

  • Evaluate each nomination fairly and impartially, based solely on the merits of the nominee's accomplishments and the quality of the supporting materials.
  • Avoid any bias or preconceptions based on the nominee's or nominator's institution, background, or personal characteristics.

Technical Requirements:

  • Access the nominations and submit your evaluations through our secure online judging portal.
  • Ensure that you have a stable internet connection and that your browser is up-to-date.

By adhering to these requirements and guidelines, you will help us maintain a rigorous and equitable evaluation process. Thank you for your dedication and commitment to recognizing and celebrating excellence in our community.
