Nomination Requirements
Before you proceed with the nomination form, please ensure that you and your nominee meet the following requirements to guarantee a successful submission.

    Eligibility
  • Nominees must be actively involved or have made significant contributions in the field/industry pertaining to the award category within the past year.
  • Nominators should have direct knowledge of the nominee's work and accomplishments.
  • Nominators must provide their contact information for verification purposes.
    • Nomination Materials
  • A completed nomination form with accurate and detailed information about the nominee's achievements.
  • A brief biography of the nominee (maximum 300 words).
  • At least one letter of support from someone other than the nominator, highlighting the nominee's qualifications and impact (maximum 500 words). Additional letters of support are welcome but not required.
