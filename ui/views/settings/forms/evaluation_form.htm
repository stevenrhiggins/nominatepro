Select the type of scale you would like judges to use. You can choose either a Likert scale or a numeric scale.

  1. The Likert scale allows respondents to specify their level of agreement or disagreement on a symmetric agree-disagree scale for a series of statements or questions. We use a 7 point scale.
  2. The numeric scale alllows respondents to select from the number 1 to whatever you select in the dropdown list below (e.g. 1 - 10 ) for a series of statements or questions.
